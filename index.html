About TACSFON ABSU

TACSFON ABSU is a vibrant community of students dedicated to spiritual growth, academic excellence, and impactful Christian fellowship. Rooted in the teachings of the Apostles, we strive to build a strong foundation of faith among students, equipping them to be ambassadors of Christ both on and off campus.

At TACSFON ABSU, we believe in the transformative power of faith and fellowship. Through regular Bible studies, prayer meetings, outreach programs, and campus events, we encourage students to live purposefully, reflecting the light of Christ in their academic journey and beyond.

Our Mission

Biblical Teaching

Empowering young believers through sound biblical teachings and principles.

Fellowship

Creating a space where students can deepen their faith and build lasting relationships.

Mentorship

Guiding students through their spiritual and academic journey with experienced mentors.

Service

Encouraging students to serve their community and be ambassadors of Christ.

Our Programs

Bible Study

Weekly in-depth study of God's word to build a strong spiritual foundation.

Prayer Meetings

Regular prayer gatherings to seek God's guidance and strengthen our faith.

Outreach Programs

Community service and evangelism to share God's love with others.

Campus Events

Special events and conferences to inspire and equip students for their journey.
